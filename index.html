Accessible Essentials Website Quiz!

Welcome to the Accessible Essentials Quiz!

Click below to begin a short quiz to find out how mindful you are of accessibility when coding your websites!

Be honest with yourself- everyone has room for improvement and there is no time like the present to get started on learning about accessible web design. After all, we want as many users enjoying the content as possible!

Please answer the following with the question in mind: When you make your websites...

Do you declare the language attribute in the opening 'html' tag of your HTML document so the screen reader knows the correct pronounciation of the page elements?

Is there consistently descriptive alt-text included with each image element?

How often do you use the :focus pseudoselector on elements that have a :hover state to make sure users navigating with their keyboards know what element is in focus?

How concious are you of text-background contrasts? Do you make sure that your aesthetically pleasing colour choices are also readable?

Do you include a "skip to main content" link as one of the first elements of your page so that users are able to avoid tabbing through, or listening to, lengthy navigations or decorative header content?
